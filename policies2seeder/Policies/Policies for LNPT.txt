Module Laporan Nilaian Prestasi Tahunan (LNPT)
#1. Purata LNPT
	#1. Purata LNPT 
		#1. Urus Setia LNPT 
			#1. Function Listing
				1. "US LNPT" can "filter" "Senarai Kakitangan" based on chosen duration of the assessment such two or even three years.
				2. "US LNPT" can "view" "Senarai Kakitangan" based on chosen duration of the assessment.
				
			#2. Function View Details
				1. "US LNPT" can "view" "Butiran Kakitangan" based on selected employee.
				2. "US LNPT" can "view" "Pengiraan Puara Mengikut Tahun Yang Dipilih".
				3. "US LNPT" can "download" "laporan LNPT". 
		#2. System 
			1. System can "calculate" "Purata Mengikut Tahun Yang Dipilih".
			2. System can "generate" "laporan LNPT".
			
	#2. Purata LNPT bagi pengesahan dalam lantikan.
		#1. Urus Setia LNPT 
			#1. Function Listing
				1. "US LNPT" can "filter" "Senarai Kakitangan" based on chosen duration of the assessment.
				2. "US LNPT" can "view" "Senarai Kakitangan" based on chosen duration of the assessment.
				
			#2. Function View Details
				1. "US LNPT" can "view" "Butiran Kakitangan" based on selected employee.
				2. "US LNPT" can "view" "Pengiraan Puara 2 Tahun (36.4%,63.6%)".
				3. "US LNPT" can "download" "laporan LNPT 2 Tahun". 
		#2. System 
			1. System can "calculate" "Purata Mengikut Tahun Yang Dipilih".
			2. System can "generate" "laporan LNPT 2 Tahun".
			
#2. Sejarah APC (Anugerah Pekerja Cemerlang)
	#1. Sejarah APC bagi kakitangan LKIM 
		#1. Urus Setia
			#1. Function Listing	
				1. "US LNPT" can "filter" "Senarai Penerima APC".
			#2. Function View Details
				1. "US LNPT" can "view" "Butiran Penerima APC" for selected employee.
				2. "US LNPT" can "update" "Maklumat Tahun APC".
				3. "US LNPT" can "download" "Laporan APC".
				
		#2. System
			1. System "update" "Maklumat Kakitangan".
			2. System "generate" "Laporan APC".
